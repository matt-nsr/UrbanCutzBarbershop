Our Story

Family-Owned, Street-Built

Urban Cutz started as one chair in Mirdif and grew into three doors across Dubai, Sharjah and Ajman, without losing the walk-in, family-first feel that got it there. We're known for handling afro & textured hair with real skill, and for a fair price and a welcome that doesn't feel corporate.

Today that means the region's go-to spot for skin fades and braids, and, uniquely, full hair fixing and toupee work done same-day, in a shop built to feel like a night out, not a waiting room.

Good to Know

Quick Questions

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?

Both work. We're walk-in friendly at every branch, but booking ahead on Fresha or WhatsApp guarantees your slot, especially evenings and weekends.

What payment methods do you accept?

Cash is accepted in-store at every branch. Some branches may also take card, message the branch on WhatsApp beforehand if you want to confirm before you arrive.

What's the price range for a cut?

Most services fall between 30 and 95 AED depending on the cut and branch. See the Services & Pricing section above for the full list, or ask on WhatsApp for exact same-day pricing.

Do you offer home service?

Yes, full home service is available anywhere in Dubai, Sharjah or Ajman. Message us on WhatsApp with your location to book.

Are kids welcome?

Absolutely, we're a family-friendly shop. Kids cuts are on the menu and there's usually something to keep them busy while they wait.

Do you do hair fixing, toupees or braids at every branch?

Yes, hair fixing, toupees and braid work are offered at all three branches. Message us on WhatsApp with the branch you'd like so we can confirm barber availability.

Can I book a specific branch on Fresha?

Mirdif has its own Fresha page, use the "Fresha" link on that branch's card. For Muwaileh, Sharjah and Al Jerf 2, Ajman, book directly via WhatsApp or call for now.

Which branch should I choose?

Whichever is closest, all three run the same menu and standard. Mirdif keeps Dubai hours (11am to 11pm), while Muwaileh and Al Jerf 2 run later (12pm to 12am).
